Amit Sheth(http://knoesis.org/amit) is the LexisNexis Ohio Eminent Scholar at Wright State University, Dayton, Ohio. He directs the Center of Excellence on Knowledge-enabled Human Centered Computing (Knucomp) which includes the Kno.e.sis Center. His recent work are in Semantic Web/Web3.0, including semantics-empowered Services/Cloud Computing, Sensor and Social computing over Web and mobile platforms. Prof. Sheth is an IEEE fellow, is among the highly-cited authors in computer science (h index = 63; 39 with 100+ citations each). He has given more than 220 invited talks including 38 keynotes, is EIC of the Intl. Journal of Semantic Web & Information Systems, is joint-EIC of Distributed & Parallel Databases, and serves on several editorial boards. By licensing his funded university research, he has also founded and managed two successful companies. Several commercial products and many operationally deployed applications have resulted from his R&D.
