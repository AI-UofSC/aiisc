Research Areas

Data/Information/Knowledge Integration and Analysis; Semantic Web and Semantic Applications (knowledge discovery and intermediation, multi-ontology and complex relations, search/directory/personalization/analytics, business intelligence, etc.), Management of Rich Media Content and Information Sources, Digital Libraries, Pan-Web and Enterprise Information Interoperability (incl. federated information systems, multi-agent and information brokering architectures, ontology and metadata management), Web and Business Processes (Semantic Web Services/Processes, Workflow Management, Autonomic/Adaptable/Dynamic Web/Business Processes, Semantic Social Networks, eCommerce/eService/eScience). Significant collaborations and applications in Bioinformatics/Life Science, Health Care, GIS, Financial, and National Security domains.
